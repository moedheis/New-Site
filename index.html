The Two Traps Killing

Before I show you the fix, let's look at what's really going wrong. Most contractors are losing jobs for one of two reasons. Maybe both.

Problem 1
The Shared Lead Trap
Sites like Angi and HomeAdvisor sell that same homeowner's phone number to 3 to 5 other contractors. The second they hit submit. You're racing four other guys to be the first one to call back. You already paid for a lead you have almost no shot at winning.
